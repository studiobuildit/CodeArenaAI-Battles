# Battle 26 - Helicopter Rescue

## Prompt

Create a 2D helicopter rescue game that plays itself. A helicopter flies across the top with spinning rotor blades. It lowers a rope to pick up stranded people on rooftops of buildings surrounded by rising blue flood water. Water level slowly rises. Helicopter picks up one person at a time and flies them to safety on the left. Rain falls constantly. Lightning flashes occasionally. Show rescue counter. Continuous auto-play loop.
